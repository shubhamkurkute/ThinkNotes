
#### Image Processing
- It is the process of transforming an image into a new one that is clearer and easier to analyze.
- It encorporates image acquisition, enhancement, restoration, and segmentation
- A few important aspects of the image enhancement and restoration:
	- Removing of the noise
	- Dealing with motion blur
	- Removing defocus blur
	- Enhancing edges and corners
